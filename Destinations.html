Sri Dalada Maligawa

Sri Dalada Maligawa is a sacred Buddhist temple in Kandy that houses the Tooth Relic of Lord Buddha and is a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Anuradhapura

Anuradhapura is an ancient capital of Sri Lanka, known for its well-preserved ruins of ancient civilization and sacred Buddhist sites.

Polonnaruwa

Polonnaruwa is a UNESCO World Heritage city with remarkable ruins, ancient temples, and stone statues showcasing Sri Lanka’s royal history.

Sigiriya Rock Fortress

Ancient rock fortress and UNESCO World Heritage Site.
